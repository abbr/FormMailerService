Help

FormMailer service e-mails the forms filled out by your site user to the addresses you configured. You design the forms at your web site's end and set the post destination to the URL provided by FormMailer service. FormMailer service will take care of the email part of the workflow. To complete the workflow, design a success and failure message page at your web site's end. The success or failure message is displayed by one of two means:
  • If the forms are posted in AJAX mode, leave the the success and failure page URL fields empty. The service uses HTTP response code to indicate the outcome to your site's AJAX client.
  • In non-AJAX mode, supply the URL of the success and failure page in configuration.
